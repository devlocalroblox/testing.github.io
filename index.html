About

Building

I make builds that are easy on the eyes and still feel right for gameplay. That can mean smaller detailed areas, scene pieces, or larger map sections depending on what the project needs. I care a lot about shape, flow, and making the final result feel worth using in an actual game.

Animating

I also create Roblox animations with a focus on smooth movement, readable posing, and motion that fits the style of the game. The goal is never to overdo it; just clean animation work that feels polished and natural once it’s in game.
